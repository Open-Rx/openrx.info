OpenRx is a harm reduction and sourcing guide for DIY pharmaceuticals, focusing
on DIY HRT and DIY GLP-1 medication. It exists as an eternal, harassment-
resistant resource for those in need of life-saving medication who find
themselves fighting against a system that was built against them, whether that
be at home, with insurance, with their government fighting against their 
right to be who they are, or at any other stage of their self-actualization
journey.

We pride ourselves on being as harassment-resilient as our situation allows us
to be, with a focus on decentralizing our efforts in the future in order to
expand that capability. 

This website exists as a resource for you, and for us. Any purchases made at
vendors mentioned here, and any dosing performed based on the information
posted here, as well as generally every decision made as a result of the
information on this website is made of your own volition, and at your own risk.
Please do your own research where necessary, talk to licensed care providers
where possible, and above all be safe, be smart, and be you.
