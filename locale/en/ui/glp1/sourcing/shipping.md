## Shipping from China

Most gray-market GLP-1 peptide vendors ship from China. Here's what to expect.

### Key Facts

- Each vial of peptide weighs well under 250 g and is declared at less than $800, so it clears US Customs duty-free under Section 321 / Entry Type 86.
- **Typical transit:** 7 days (best case) · 12 days (average) · 21 days (maximum)

### The Shipping Chain

1. Factory courier in China
2. Export freight forwarder (YunExpress, 4PX, Yanwen, etc.)
3. US shipping partner / postal consolidator (ACI Logistix, UniUni, DHL eCommerce, etc.)
4. Domestic carrier — usually USPS, sometimes FedEx Ground Economy or UPS Mail Innovations
5. Your regular mail carrier

### US Carrier Breakdown

| Service booked in China | Stateside path | Last-mile carrier |
|---|---|---|
| Dedicated-line Parcel | USPS Ground Advantage label | USPS |
| FedEx Ground Economy | Entirely FedEx network | FedEx driver |
| UPS Mail Innovations | UPS middle-mile, then USPS | USPS / UPS |
| DHL eCommerce | DHL sort hub → USPS | USPS |

### Tracking

Use **ParcelsApp.com** with the original China tracking ID (starts with `YT…`, `LP…`, etc.). It automatically follows the parcel through the Chinese export leg, the US partner hub, and the final domestic carrier.

If tracking shows "USPS Awaiting Item" for more than five days after the first US scan, open a ticket with the shipping partner named in the tracking (ACI, UniUni, DHL eCommerce, etc.) — they are responsible for injecting the sack into the carrier network.

### Quick Recap

- Typical delivery: 12 days, but can be 7–21 days
- "Shipping Partner – USPS Awaiting Item" = USPS has the label but not the parcel yet; it's still at the partner hub
- Once you see "Accepted at USPS facility," delivery is usually 2–5 days away
- Shipments stay under the $800 de-minimis limit — no US duty or tax on arrival

### Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Dedicated line | A fixed, daily charter flight reserved for e-commerce parcels |
| Section 321 / Entry Type 86 | US rule allowing packages ≤ $800 to clear customs duty-free |
| Shipping partner / consolidator | US warehouse that receives the pallet and injects it into USPS/FedEx/UPS |
| USPS Awaiting Item | USPS has the label info but not the physical parcel yet |
