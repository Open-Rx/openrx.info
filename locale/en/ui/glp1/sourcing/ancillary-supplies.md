## Sourcing Ancillary Supplies (BAC Water, Syringes, Swabs, Sharps Containers)

For Path B (Authentic Vials) and especially Path C (DIY Powder), obtaining sterile ancillary supplies is critical for safety. Sourcing these properly is arguably as important as sourcing the medication itself.

### Bacteriostatic Water

- Bacteriostatic Water for Injection, USP (30 mL) — Hospira brand, available at BacteriostaticWater.com
- Sterile Water for Injection, USP (10 mL) — Hospira brand, available at BacteriostaticWater.com

### Syringes & Needles

- **Mixing Syringes (Luer Lock):** Dynarex 3 mL — Mountainside Medical Equipment
- **Hypodermic Needles:** BD SafetyGlide, 25 G × 5/8″ — Mountainside Medical Equipment
- **Insulin Syringes:** Dynarex Easy Touch U-100, 1 mL — Mountainside Medical Equipment

### Alcohol Swabs

- BD Alcohol Swabs (70% isopropyl), 100 count — Amazon
- Curad Alcohol Prep Pads, 200 count — Amazon

### Sharps Containers

- FDA-Cleared containers — Post Medical
- Medline Sharps Disposal Containers — Medline At Home
- Travel-Size: Alcedo 1 Quart — Amazon

### Local Pharmacies

Community pharmacies (CVS, Walgreens, Rite Aid) carry insulin syringes, alcohol swabs, and sharps containers. Verify items are individually sealed and labeled "sterile."

Insulin syringes are available without a prescription in most US states.
