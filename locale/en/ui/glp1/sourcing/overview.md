### Why Gray Market Vendors Don't Accept Credit Cards or PayPal

Most gray-market vendors require cryptocurrency because payment processors (Visa, Mastercard, PayPal) prohibit transactions for pharmaceutical products sold outside of licensed channels. Crypto transactions are irreversible and pseudonymous, making them the default payment method.
