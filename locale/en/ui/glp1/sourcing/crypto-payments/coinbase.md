## Using Coinbase

Coinbase is a major US-based exchange requiring identity verification (KYC). It supports BTC, USDC, USDT, and many other assets.

### Set Up and Verify Your Account

1. Go to coinbase.com or download the Coinbase app and click **Get started / Sign up**
2. Create an account with your email and a strong password
3. Complete identity verification: provide your name, address, date of birth, and government ID
4. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

### Link a Payment Method

1. Click your profile icon → **Settings** → **Payment methods** (desktop), or tap **Profile** → **Payment methods** (mobile)
2. Add a **bank account (ACH)** or **debit card** — credit cards are generally not supported for purchases
3. Wait for verification (ACH takes 1–3 days; debit cards are instant)

### Buy Crypto

1. Click **Buy / Sell** in the top menu (desktop) or tap **Buy** on the home screen (mobile)
2. Select the cryptocurrency you need
3. Enter the amount in USD or crypto units
4. Choose your linked payment method
5. Click **Preview Buy** → review fees and total cost → **Buy now**

### Send Crypto to the Vendor

1. Click **Send / Receive** → **Send** (desktop) or tap **Send** (mobile)
2. Choose the asset to send
3. Tap **Network** and select the correct blockchain (e.g., ERC-20 for USDC)
4. Paste or scan the vendor's exact wallet address — double-check first and last four characters
5. Enter the exact amount
6. Review network fees and click **Send now**

> ⚠️ Selecting the correct network is crucial for stablecoins. Sending on the wrong chain will likely result in permanent loss of funds. Always verify with your vendor.
