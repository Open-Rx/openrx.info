## Using Cash App (Bitcoin)

Cash App is known for its simplicity in buying and sending Bitcoin (BTC). Before you start, ensure your Cash App is fully verified and you have a linked funding source.

### Buy Bitcoin on Cash App

1. Open Cash App and tap the Bitcoin tile on the home screen
2. Tap **Buy**
3. Enter the USD amount or BTC amount desired
4. Choose your funding source (linked bank account or Cash App balance)
5. Confirm with PIN or Touch/Face ID
6. Tap **Confirm** — BTC appears in your Cash App Bitcoin balance

### Send Bitcoin to an External Wallet

1. Tap the Bitcoin tile on the home screen
2. Tap **Send** (paper-airplane icon)
3. Enter the BTC amount to send
4. Paste or scan the vendor's wallet address — verify the first and last 4 characters
5. Tap **Next** and choose network speed
6. Review transaction details carefully
7. Confirm with PIN or Touch/Face ID

> ⚠️ Bitcoin transactions are irreversible. Always verify the address and amount before sending.
