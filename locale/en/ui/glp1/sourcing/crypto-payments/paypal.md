## Using PayPal (US Users)

PayPal's built-in cryptocurrency feature lets you buy, sell, and send supported assets (BTC, ETH, LTC, BCH, SOL, LINK).

### Set Up and Access Crypto Hub

1. Log in to your PayPal account on web or mobile
2. Tap **Crypto** in the Finances tab (mobile) or select **Cryptocurrency** on the web
3. Complete identity verification if prompted

### Buy Crypto on PayPal

1. In the Crypto section, tap **Buy** (mobile) or **Buy / Sell** (web)
2. Select your cryptocurrency (e.g., Bitcoin)
3. Enter the USD amount
4. Choose your funding source (PayPal balance, linked bank, or debit card)
5. Review fees and click **Buy now** or **Confirm Purchase**

### Send Crypto to an External Wallet

1. Go to Crypto and tap **Send** (mobile) or **Transfer crypto** (web)
2. Paste the recipient's wallet address — double-check characters
3. Select asset and amount
4. Review network fees and details
5. Confirm by tapping **Send**

> ⚠️ Crypto transfers on PayPal may be subject to a 48-hour security review.
