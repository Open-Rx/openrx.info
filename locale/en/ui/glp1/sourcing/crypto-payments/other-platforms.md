## Other Platforms (Kraken, Gemini & MoonPay)

These additional on-ramps all support buying and sending common assets like BTC, ETH, and stablecoins.

### Kraken

**Buying crypto:**
1. Create an account at kraken.com and verify your email
2. Complete identity verification (KYC) by uploading your ID
3. Navigate to **Funding → Deposit**, select your fiat currency, and follow the bank transfer instructions
4. After funds clear, go to **Trade → New Order**, select your trading pair (e.g., BTC/USD), enter amount, and click **Buy**

**Sending crypto:**
1. Go to **Funding → Withdraw**
2. Select the asset and add the vendor's wallet address with the correct network
3. Enter the amount and review the network fee
4. Confirm and approve via email or 2FA

### Gemini

**Buying crypto:**
1. Sign up at gemini.com or install the Gemini app; verify email and set a password
2. Complete KYC by uploading your ID
3. Click **Transfer Funds → Add Funds** and choose ACH or Wire Transfer
4. Once funded, go to **Market** or tap **Buy**; select your asset, enter amount, and confirm

**Withdrawing crypto:**
1. Select **Transfer Funds → Withdraw Crypto**
2. Choose the cryptocurrency and add the vendor's address
3. Input the amount and review any withdrawal hold or fees
4. Confirm via Authy 2FA within 4 minutes

### MoonPay

**Buying crypto:**
1. Open the MoonPay widget or a partnered wallet (look for "Powered by MoonPay")
2. Select the cryptocurrency and enter the USD amount
3. Choose payment method (credit/debit card, bank transfer, Apple Pay, PayPal)
4. Enter payment details and complete the purchase

> ⚠️ All transactions are irreversible. Double-check addresses, amounts, and networks before sending.
