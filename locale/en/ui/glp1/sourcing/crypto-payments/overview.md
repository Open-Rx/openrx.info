## Crypto Payments: Introduction & Concepts

Most US-based crypto platforms require identity verification (KYC). Funding methods include bank account (ACH), debit cards, credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

### Key Warnings About Card Usage

- **Bank declines are common**, particularly with credit cards — financial institutions often block crypto transactions
- **Debit cards perform better** than credit cards for crypto purchases
- **Credit card costs are high:** exchange processing fees (~3%+) plus potential cash advance fees from your card issuer (another 3–5%+)
- Apple Pay/Google Pay don't eliminate the underlying card problems or fees

### Recommended Method

Bank account transfers (ACH) are slower but typically involve lower fees and better reliability.
