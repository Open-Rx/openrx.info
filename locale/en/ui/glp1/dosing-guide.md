## Dosing Guide

GLP-1 RA therapy follows one overriding rule: **start low, go slow.** These are the standard dosing schedules used in clinical practice and supported by trial data.

### Semaglutide

Initiation: 0.25 mg subcutaneous once weekly for weeks 1–4. After week 4, increase to 0.5 mg. Continue titrating in 4-week intervals based on tolerance up to a maximum of 2 mg (Ozempic®) or 2.4 mg (Wegovy®) weekly.

### Tirzepatide

Initiation: 2.5 mg subcutaneous once weekly for weeks 1–4. After week 4, increase to 5 mg. Continue titrating in 4-week intervals up to a maximum of 15 mg (Mounjaro®) weekly if needed and tolerated.

### Retatrutide

Initiation: 1–2 mg subcutaneous once weekly (extrapolated from clinical trial data). Increase by 1–2 mg every 4 weeks. Therapeutic range is 5–15 mg weekly based on clinical data. Gray market vials may contain up to 30 mg — use extreme caution.

> ⚠️ Doses above 15 mg for retatrutide exceed those tested in published clinical trials.

### Injection Administration

1. Prepare medication per your path guide (A, B, or C)
2. Choose and clean an injection site (abdomen, thigh, or upper arm)
3. Administer subcutaneous injection per your path guide
4. Dispose of sharps safely in an approved container

### If You Experience Side Effects

Consider extending your current dose for an additional 4 weeks before titrating up. If side effects are severe, reduce to the previous dose. Only a qualified healthcare provider should make final decisions about dose adjustments.

> Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting, changing, or stopping any medication.
