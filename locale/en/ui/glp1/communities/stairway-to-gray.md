## Stairway to Gray: How to Find Them (& Is It Right for You?)

Stairway to Gray (STG) is a large Telegram community focused on DIY peptide research for experienced users. It has 35,000+ members and is one of the most active peptide communities online.

**Website:** stairwaytogray.com

### Is STG Right for You?

**✅ You're ready for STG if you:**
- Have successfully reconstituted peptides before
- Understand dosage calculations
- Know bacteriostatic water and sterile technique
- Can vet vendors independently
- Are comfortable reading research papers
- Have injection experience
- Understand the legal gray market landscape

**❌ STG is NOT right for you yet if you:**
- Have never used peptides before
- Don't know reconstitution processes
- Need step-by-step guidance
- Are looking for ready-to-use products
- Are just starting GLP-1 research

### STG vs. Gray Guide Philosophy

**STG:** "Figure it out yourself." Fast-paced, advanced discussions, no hand-holding, 35,000+ members.

**Gray Guide (this guide):** "We'll help you learn." Step-by-step explanations, welcoming atmosphere, fundamental coverage.

### Recommended Learning Path

**Phase 1: Start here (2–6 months)**
- Master safe injection technique
- Learn dosage calculations
- Practice reconstitution
- Understand vendor vetting
- Build confidence

**Phase 2: Graduate to STG**
- After 3+ peptide cycles completed
- After 10+ vials reconstituted
- When you can calculate doses without help
- When you understand sterile technique intuitively

### Quick FAQ

**Can I join both?** Yes. Many people stay in Gray Guide even after joining STG to help new members.

**What does "NO OILS" mean?** STG focuses strictly on water-based peptide reconstitution. "Oils" refers to oil-based injectables (anabolic steroids) which are outside STG's scope.

**How long before I'm ready?** If actively researching and testing, probably 2–6 months. You'll know you're ready when basic questions start to bore you and you want deeper, scientific discussions.
