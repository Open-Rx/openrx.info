## Troubleshooting (Path C)

**Powder won't dissolve**

Verify that you added the correct volume of BAC water. Continue gentle swirling or rolling — full dissolution can take several minutes. Never shake. If particles remain after patient mixing, assume the product is poor quality and discard it safely.

**Cloudy solution or particles**

Do not inject. Cloudiness or sediment suggests contamination, degradation, or low-quality peptide. Discard the vial in a sharps container.

**Dosage calculation concerns**

> ⚠️ **Stop. Do not proceed if you are unsure about your dosage calculation.** Check the numbers with more than one reliable online calculator and seek input from experienced users (evaluate advice critically). If any doubt remains, do not inject. A dosing error can cause serious harm.
