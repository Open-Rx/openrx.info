## Instructions for Use (Storage, Inspection, Administration)

Always inspect products carefully upon receipt. Check the packaging for damage, verify the product name and dose, and check the expiry date. For injectables, visually examine the liquid before each use: it should typically be clear and colorless (Tizaro may be slightly yellow). Discard if cloudy, discolored, contains particles, or if the device appears damaged or expired. Never use medication suspected of having been frozen.

### Pen Injector

**Step 1: Choose an injection area**

Self-injection sites: abdomen or front/outer thigh. Helper injection site: back of upper arm. Each week rotate to a new spot — at least two finger-widths from the last puncture.

**Step 2: Remove the base cap**

1. Make sure the lock ring shows the locked symbol.
2. Grip the gray (or white) base cap and pull it straight off. Throw the cap away.
3. Do not touch the hidden needle or try to replace the cap.

**Step 3: Place, unlock, inject**

1. Press the clear base flat against your cleaned skin.
2. Turn the lock ring until the unlocked symbol lines up.
3. Push and hold the injection button until you hear two clicks and the internal plunger stops moving (up to 10 seconds).

**Step 4: Finish safely**

Lift the pen straight off the skin. The gray plunger should now fill the window. Drop the entire pen into an FDA-cleared sharps container — no recapping, no re-use.

### Pre-Filled Syringe

1. Wash hands. Remove one syringe from carton by holding barrel, not plunger or needle cap. Inspect liquid — use only if clear and particle-free.
2. Wipe the injection site with an alcohol swab. Let it air-dry completely.
3. Hold syringe barrel in the middle. Pull needle cap straight off without twisting. Discard cap immediately.
4. Pinch a fold of skin. Insert needle at approximately 45-degree angle into the skin fold.
5. Release pinched skin. Push plunger slowly and steadily until it stops and barrel is empty.
6. Remove needle at same angle as insertion. Drop entire syringe with needle attached into sharps container.
7. Choose a new injection area each week.

### Oral Semaglutide (Rybelsus®)

1. Fast for at least 8 hours before taking the tablet. Take first thing in the morning for best results.
2. Swallow tablet whole with no more than 120 mL (about half a cup) of plain water. Do not split, crush, or chew the tablet.
3. Wait 30 minutes before eating, drinking anything other than water, or taking other oral medicines or supplements.
4. Take the tablet the same way and at roughly the same time each day for optimal absorption and effectiveness.
