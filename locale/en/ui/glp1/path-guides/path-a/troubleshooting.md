## Troubleshooting (Path A)

**Dropped device**

Inspect the pen or syringe closely. If you see any crack, bend, or other damage, do not use it. See the [Safe Disposal Guide](/glp-1/safe-disposal).

**Liquid looks wrong**

Do not inject if the solution is cloudy, oddly colored (a faint yellow tint can be normal for some products), contains particles, or may have been frozen.

**Leak after injection**

A few drops on the skin usually mean the dose was not fully delivered — often because the pen was lifted too soon or the syringe plunger was not pushed to the end. Do not take an extra dose.

**Device will not fire or plunger sticks**

If the button jams, the plunger will not move, or any part seems broken, discard the device and use a new one.
