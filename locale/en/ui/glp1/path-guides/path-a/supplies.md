## Supplies Needed & Sourcing (Path A)

- **Medication** — A ready-to-use product: pen injector, pre-filled syringe pack, or oral tablets (e.g., Rybelsus®) from a gray-market vendor.
- **For injectables (pen or syringe):**
  - Alcohol swabs for cleaning skin before each injection
  - Sharps container for safe disposal of used pens or syringes

Alcohol swabs and sharps containers are sold at most local pharmacies or reputable online medical-supply stores.

Follow the [Safe Disposal Guide](/glp-1/safe-disposal) for sharps and unused medication.
