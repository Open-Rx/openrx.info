## Detailed Injection Technique & Site Rotation (Pen/Syringe)

### Site Selection

Choose a spot with a good fat layer: abdomen (at least two inches from the navel), front or outer thigh, or the back of the upper arm. Avoid bony areas and joints.

### Rotation Guidelines

Rotating sites prevents lumps (lipohypertrophy) and keeps absorption steady.

- Divide large areas into smaller sections
- Maintain 1–2 inch spacing from previous injections
- Rotate between general regions weekly
- Keep a log or calendar
- Avoid moles, scars, stretch marks, bruises, and any skin that is red, broken, infected, or hardened

### Injection Angle

- **Pens:** Hold flat against the skin (90 degrees)
- **Syringes:** 90 degrees, or 45 degrees if you have very little subcutaneous fat

### Post-Injection Care

Apply light pressure with cotton if a drop of blood appears. Do not rub.
