## Troubleshooting (Path B)

**Cannot draw the dose**

Inject the same volume of air into the vial first (see Preparation & Use). Keep the needle tip below the liquid surface. Swap to a new needle if flow still stalls — a factory clog is rare but possible.

**Too many air bubbles**

Flick the barrel so bubbles rise to the needle hub. Push the plunger slowly to vent only the air back into the vial. Tiny "champagne" bubbles are harmless — remove large bubbles before setting the final dose line.

**Questionable vial or liquid**

Do not inject if the vial is cracked, the seal is broken, or the solution looks cloudy, discolored, frozen, or expired. See the [Safe Disposal Guide](/glp-1/safe-disposal).
