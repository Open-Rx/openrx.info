## Storage (Authentic Vial)

### Unopened Vials

- Keep in a refrigerator between **2 °C and 8 °C (36 °F – 46 °F)**
- Leave each vial inside its carton to protect it from light
- **Do not freeze.** If a vial has been frozen, throw it away
- An unopened vial may stay at room temperature (below **30 °C / 86 °F**) for up to **21 days**; record the date you take it out of the fridge and use or discard it within that window

### After First Use

Mounjaro® tirzepatide vials are **single-dose**. Draw the full contents into a syringe, inject, and discard the empty vial — do not save leftovers or re-enter the vial.

### Transport Tips

- Use an insulated pouch with an ice pack for trips longer than a few minutes
- Keep the vial upright to prevent the solution from wetting the rubber stopper
- Do not expose to direct sunlight or leave in a hot car
