## Verifying Authenticity (Mounjaro® Vials)

Every Mounjaro® carton (whether shipped to India, the US, or anywhere else) carries a unique 2-D barcode that you can confirm directly with Eli Lilly.

### Step-by-Step Check

1. **Scan the carton** — Open the camera on your phone or go to `scan.lilly.com`. Point it at the square 2-D barcode printed on the outer carton (not the vial).
2. **Read the result** — Lilly's site instantly reports whether the serial number is valid, recalled, or unknown.
3. **Match carton to vial** — Product name, strength, lot number, and expiry date should match. Flip-off cap and crimp seal must be intact.
4. **Inspect the liquid** — The solution inside the vial should be clear to slightly yellow, with no particles or cloudiness.
5. **When in doubt, throw it out** — If the scan fails, packaging looks tampered, or the liquid appears abnormal, do not use the vial.

### Quick Tips

- Scan every new carton before first use
- Keep screenshots of successful scans for records
- Store vials at 2–8 °C; discard if frozen or overheated
