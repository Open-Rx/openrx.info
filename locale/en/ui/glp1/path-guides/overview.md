## Path-Specific Guides

This section provides detailed procedural information for each of the three paths, with varying steps, equipment needs, and risk levels. Before diving in, make sure you have reviewed the [universal safety principles](/glp-1/safety) and [dosing warnings](/glp-1/safety/dosing).

### Jump to Your Path

- **[Path A: Ready-to-Use](/glp-1/path-guides/path-a)** — Pens, pre-filled syringes, tablets. Minimal preparation required.
- **[Path B: Authentic Vial](/glp-1/path-guides/path-b)** — Branded Mounjaro® medication. Requires drawing doses from a vial.
- **[Path C: DIY Powder](/glp-1/path-guides/path-c)** — Lyophilized peptide powder. Highest risk, requires reconstitution. ⚠️ HIGH RISK
