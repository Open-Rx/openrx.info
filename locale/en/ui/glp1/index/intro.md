## Can't Afford Ozempic or Mounjaro?

This guide covers how to source GLP-1 receptor agonists (semaglutide, tirzepatide, retatrutide) through the gray market — legally and as safely as possible.

GLP-1 medications are genuinely life-changing for many people, but the price tags in the US are staggering. A gray market exists that allows access to the same or equivalent medications at a fraction of the cost.

**The Gray Market** refers to goods that are genuine products sold through unofficial channels. Gray market GLP-1 medications are real pharmaceutical products — just sourced outside the standard US distribution system.

**Gray Market ≠ Black Market.** Black market goods are counterfeit, illegal, or both. Gray market goods are typically genuine products imported through channels that bypass official distribution agreements. Importing small quantities of medication for personal use exists in a legal gray zone in the US — technically not permitted, but rarely if ever prosecuted for personal-use quantities.
