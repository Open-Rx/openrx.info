## Affordable prescriptions (for USA residents):

If your preferred provider doesn’t cover the cost of medications, check out **[goodrx.com](https://www.goodrx.com/)**, a completely free, no strings attached way to get coupons for prescription drugs. These services don’t prescribe medication, they just allow you to obtain medication that has already been prescribed to you at a lower cost.

**[Cost Plus Drugs](https://costplusdrugs.com/medications/)** is also a good USA source for affordable, prescription medications that are shipped to you, with a doctor’s prescription.

These resources are only available to people in the USA.
