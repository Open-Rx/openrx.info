## PayPal (US)

PayPal lets you buy and send Bitcoin, ETH, and a handful of other coins directly from your account. It's an easy option if you already have PayPal set up with a linked bank or card.

### Getting to the crypto section

On mobile, tap the Finances tab and look for Crypto. On the web, look for Cryptocurrency in the menu. You may need to complete identity verification if you haven't already.

### Buying crypto

Tap Buy, pick the coin you want, enter the dollar amount, choose your funding source, and confirm. PayPal shows you the fees before you confirm.

### Sending to a vendor

Go to Crypto and tap Send (mobile) or Transfer crypto (web). Paste the vendor's wallet address, check it carefully, select the asset and amount, review the fees, and confirm.

One thing to know: PayPal may hold crypto transfers for up to 48 hours for security review. If you're on a deadline, factor that in.
