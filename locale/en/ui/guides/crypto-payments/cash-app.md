## Cash App (Bitcoin)

Cash App is probably the easiest way to buy and send Bitcoin in the US. You'll need a verified account with a linked bank or debit card before you can buy.

### Buying Bitcoin

Open the app and tap the Bitcoin tile on the home screen. Tap Buy, enter the amount you want, pick your funding source, and confirm with your PIN or biometrics. The BTC shows up in your Cash App balance pretty much immediately.

### Sending to a vendor

Tap the Bitcoin tile, then Send. Enter the amount, paste the vendor's wallet address, and check that the first and last few characters match what the vendor gave you. Tap Next, pick a network speed (standard is fine), review the details, and confirm.

Bitcoin transactions are irreversible. Double-check the address and amount before you hit confirm.
