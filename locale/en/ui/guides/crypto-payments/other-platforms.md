## Other Options (Kraken, Gemini, MoonPay)

These are a few more ways to buy crypto if Cash App, PayPal, or Coinbase don't work for you. All of them require identity verification.

### Kraken

Deposit fiat through Funding > Deposit, then trade once the funds clear via Trade > New Order. To send, go to Funding > Withdraw, select the asset, add the vendor's address with the right network, and confirm through email or 2FA.

### Gemini

Fund your account through Transfer Funds > Add Funds (ACH or wire transfer). Once funded, use the Market or Buy screen to buy. To withdraw, go to Transfer Funds > Withdraw Crypto, add the vendor's address, and confirm via Authy 2FA. Note that you have about 4 minutes to confirm after initiating a withdrawal.

### MoonPay

MoonPay is a buy-only widget embedded in a lot of wallets and apps — look for "Powered by MoonPay." You pick the coin, enter the amount, pay with a card or bank transfer, and it sends directly to a wallet address. No separate exchange account needed.

---

Regardless of which platform you use, crypto transactions are irreversible once confirmed. Verify the address, the coin, and the network before you send.
