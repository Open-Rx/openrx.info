#### France

You can reach out to [https://safe.asso.fr/index.php/rdr-a-distance](https://safe.asso.fr/index.php/rdr-a-distance) for help with finding supplies. This service was established for harm reduction by providing safe and sterile drug use equipment, but their services overlap with HRT injections – meaning they will provide supplies free of charge.


#### Ireland/Scotland

You can reach out to [Trans Harm Reduction](https://transharmreduction.org/get-injection-supplies) has some resources for people who need injection supplies.

There is a form for Ireland residents if they need free injection supplies, and Trans Harm Reduction can also help you fund needle purchases if you email them (you must be living in Ireland or Scotland).

The links on the page to Irish and Scottish needle exchanges unfortunately are not working as of the time of writing.
