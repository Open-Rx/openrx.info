## Dosing:

#### Note on HRT dosing:

**Any regimen:**
There’s no strong evidence that starting with a low estradiol dose and slowly increasing it is better than starting at a moderate dose. Some anecdotes suggest it may improve breast development or feminization, but these claims lack clinical support. While generally not harmful, lower starting doses may make testosterone suppression more difficult. You can experiment if you choose.

**For regimens with antiandrogens:**
There is usually no reason to start HRT on a lower than standard dose of antiandrogen, you should go directly onto a typical dose of antiandrogen and adjust accordingly if needed.

**For regimens with oral or sublingual/buccal estradiol:**
To dose pills accurately, you may need a pill cutter, which you can find on Amazon or at most pharmacies. Check your HRT pill type to see how it should be split to match your dose. For example, if you take 2 mg of estradiol three times a day but only have 4 mg pills, you’ll need to cut each pill in half.
