## Introduction to DIY HRT:

In countries like the USA, UK, Canada, and Australia, accessing DIY testosterone is typically *easier* and *less expensive* compared to DIY estrogen.

This is due to the significant market for testosterone among cisgender men using it for bodybuilding or performance enhancement, leading to established, trusted sources within this community.

The kind of testosterone used by trans masculine individuals for hormone therapy is the same testosterone available through these sources.
